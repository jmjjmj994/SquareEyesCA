Thank you for visiting SquareEyes!
Our vision is to deliver high quality films on demand, and our goal is to connect film-lovers around the world.

We offer a high selection of films from well-known filmmakers as well as interesting movies from small, up-and-coming filmmakers.

SquareEyes is a non-profit website and the money you spend on films here, goes directly to the people who made them.
